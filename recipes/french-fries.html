French Fries

Description

I love the corn dogs you buy at carnivals but wanted to make them at home so I created this easy batter for frankfurters. Great served with mustard.

Ingridients

  • 500 gm potato
  • 2 cup refined oil
  • salt as required

Steps

  1. Chop the potatoes.
  2. Soak cut potatoes in ice-cold water for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Deep fry the potato fries.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper and serve hot.
